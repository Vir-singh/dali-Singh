Ram Ghat

Ram Ghat is a popular and sacred destination located in the state of Uttar Pradesh. It is situated on the banks of the Mandakini River.

Sabari Waterfall

Shabari waterfall (Shabari Jal Prapat) has three water bodies that fall from 40 feet parallelly, followed by a 60-foot-wide water bed (pool) and one more waterfall that leads to a larger water body that disappears in the forest.
